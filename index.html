• BitGadaa is an experimental new media art project created by N.D. Jebessa c. 2013/14.
  • BitGadaa envisioned a futuristic African democracy inspired by Bitcoin and the Gadaa system.
  • BitGadaa explored the technoculture & philosophy of indigeneous cultural heritage and blockchain governance.
  • BitGadaa has been presented/pitched at Howard, GWU, Harvard, ODA (Ethiopia) and the Media Lab @ MIT, among others.
  • BitGadaa continues to inform and motivate its creator's entrepreneurial ventures in forging a better future with disruptive technology.
